Biography

A Neurologist of Distinction

Dr. Zulkifli K. Misri is a highly accomplished Consultant Neurologist based in Mangaluru, Karnataka. He serves as a Visiting Associate Professor of Neurology at KMC and a PhD Guide for Manipal University — one of India's premier academic institutions.

He is the President of the Karavali Chapter of Neurology, and holds membership in the Indian Epilepsy Association, Indian Stroke Association, IMA, and API. A prolific academic, Dr. Misri is the author of numerous national and international publications.
