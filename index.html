About the Doctor

Meet Dr. Z K Misri

Dr. Z K Misri is a highly accomplished senior consultant neurologist with over 30 years of clinical experience. He is an Associate Professor of Neurology at Kasturba Medical College (KMC) and serves as a PhD guide for Manipal University.

Specializing in cerebrovascular disease, stroke management, epilepsy, movement disorders, and general neurology, Dr. Misri has treated thousands of patients with complex neurological conditions. His approach combines cutting-edge medical knowledge with compassionate patient care.
